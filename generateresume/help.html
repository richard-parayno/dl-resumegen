Having a hard time to use the application? Well, here are the steps that will make your work easier. Don't worry, everything you input in the application will be confidential.



Step 1: The Basic Information is the field where the application will ask for your identity.


Step 2: The Educational Background is the field where the application will ask for the year and place where you graduate (Secondary Highschool and College) and the awards received.


Step 3: The Co-Curricular Activities is the field where you will input for the different organizations or institutions you applied for and accepted as a member or a person with a position.


Step 4: The Seminars dropdown tab enables you to input the seminars you've attended or conducted(Optional)


Step 5: The Optional Tab contains various sections which you can answer optionally.
